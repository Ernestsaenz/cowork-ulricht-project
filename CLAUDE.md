## Key Technical Facts for the Application

- **AICOMTA** = RAG-based clinical AI assistant; Gemini 2.0 Flash backbone; multilingual-e5-large embeddings; Qdrant vector DB; specialist-validated knowledge base
- LLM wrappers (like AICOMTA) are **explicitly eligible** per FAQ Q29
- EVAH defines a **Clinical Decision Support Tool (CDST)** by 5 criteria (FAQ Q22): aids clinical decisions, uses patient data, provides patient-specific recommendations, presented to clinician at point of care, does not replace clinical judgment
- Cross-geography validation (Spain → Myanmar) is **transferable** per FAQ Q27
- Before-after study designs alone are **insufficient** — need comparison group (FAQ Q39)
- PI can only submit **ONE application as lead** (FAQ Q6) — critical constraint
- Phase 1 adaptation costs may be ineligible if framed as "product development" (FAQ Q50)
- Pathway A → Pathway B progression is **explicitly encouraged** (FAQ Q35)
- Ethics approval is **NOT required before submission**, but plan must be described (FAQ Q67–Q75)
- IP stays with applicant organization; funder gets non-exclusive license (FAQ Q61–Q66)
